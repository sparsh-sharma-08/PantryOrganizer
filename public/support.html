Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my pantry?
To add items to your pantry, go to the Dashboard and click the "Add Item" button. Fill in the item details including name, quantity, unit, expiry date, and category. Click "Save" to add the item to your pantry.
How do I set up expiry reminders?
Go to Settings > Notifications and enable "Expiry Reminders". You can also set the number of days before expiry to receive warnings. The default is 2 days, but you can adjust this to 1, 3, or 7 days.
Can I export my pantry data?
Yes! Go to Settings > Data Management and click "Export". You can choose the format (JSON, CSV, or TXT) and select which data to include (items, categories, shopping lists, settings).
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings > Account and click "Change Password". Enter your current password, new password (minimum 8 characters), and confirm the new password. Click "Change Password" to save.
What if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
How do I organize items by categories?
When adding items, you can select from existing categories or create new ones. Go to Settings > Pantry Settings and enable "Auto-categorization" to automatically categorize new items based on their names.
